# Sanitized Escalation Example

## Subject

Escalation: Migration dependency blocking Q3 validation milestone

## Message

I am escalating a dependency that is putting the Q3 validation milestone at risk.

The program is blocked on access to the validation environment required for final evidence collection. The platform team has confirmed that provisioning is in progress, but we do not yet have a committed completion date. Without that date, the validation window may compress and the control evidence package may miss the planned review.

Prior resolution attempts include follow-ups in the program Slack channel, Jira comments on the dependency ticket, and direct alignment with the platform owner.

The requested support is a committed provisioning date or an approved alternate validation path by Tuesday at 12 PM. My recommendation is to keep the current milestone date for now, but treat Tuesday as the escalation point. If we do not have a path by then, we should re-plan the validation window rather than pretend it is still intact.

## Decision needed

Confirm one of the following:

1. Platform team commits to provisioning by Tuesday.
2. Engineering leadership approves an alternate validation path.
3. Program leadership accepts risk to the Q3 validation milestone.

## TPM note

This escalation is intentionally direct. It names the risk, the prior attempts, the decision needed, and the point where the plan changes.

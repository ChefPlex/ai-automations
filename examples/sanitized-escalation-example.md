# Sanitized Escalation Example

## Subject

Escalation: Migration dependency blocking Q3 validation milestone

## Message

I am escalating a dependency that is putting the Q3 validation milestone at risk.

The program is blocked on access to the validation environment required for final evidence collection. The platform team has confirmed that provisioning is in progress, but no committed completion date is currently available. Without access by Tuesday, the program will lose the planned validation buffer and may require either a compressed review window or milestone risk acceptance.

Prior resolution attempts include follow ups in the program Slack channel, Jira comments on the dependency ticket, and direct alignment with the platform owner.

The requested support is a committed provisioning date or an alternate validation path by Tuesday at 12 PM. My recommendation is to keep the current milestone date only if environment access is available by Tuesday. Otherwise, we should move to a conditional milestone plan with explicit risk acceptance.

## Decision needed

Confirm one of the following:

1. Platform team commits to provisioning by Tuesday.
2. Engineering leadership approves an alternate validation path.
3. Program leadership accepts risk to the Q3 validation milestone.
